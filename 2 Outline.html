Outline of our Stream


Elaboration


Introduction (15 min): Start the stream with an introduction of the members of the group and a brief overview of the schedule for the stream.

Game 1 (1 hour): Begin playing the first game, and provide commentary and insights as you play. Take breaks as needed for bathroom or short snack/drink.

Audience interaction (15 min): Encourage audience participation by taking questions and comments from viewers, playing mini-games with them, and have some interactive segments like trivia games or raffles with in-stream rewards.

Game 2 (1 hour): Transition to the next game, with a brief introduction of the game before starting to play.

Break (15 min): Use the break to answer questions, check the chat and interact with the audience

Game 3 (1 hour): Continue playing the third game, and provide commentary and insights as you play.

Audience interaction (15 min): Encourage audience participation by taking questions and comments from viewers, playing mini-games with them, and have some interactive segments like trivia games or raffles with in-stream rewards.

Game 4 (1 hour): Transition to the next game, with a brief introduction of the game before starting to play.

Conclusion (15 min): Wrap up the stream by summarizing the games played, announcing upcoming streams, and thanking the audience for watching.
